Démo !

How is that possible?

  • Cookies
  • iframes
  • Spying pixels
  • Every content included from other websites

What can I do?
As a user

  • Firefox settings: Block third-party cookies, DoNotTrack...
  • Automatic solutions: Ghostery / Disconnect.me, Adblock...
  • More technical solutions: NoScript, RequestPolicy...
  • But we have nothing without goodwill
Les gens n'aiment pas devoir faire attention. Comme pour leur ordinateur, ils ont envie qu'on leur dise "tu as un antivirus, maintenant tu n'auras plus jamais aucun problème."

And now?

Démo !

What can I do?
As a powerful user

  • Use alternative search engines: DuckduckGo, Ixquick/Startpage...
  • Host your own services: OwnCloud, CozyCloud...
  • Use alternative OS: GNU/Linux, Cyanogenmod/Replicant, Firefox OS...
  • http://prism-break.org
  • Spread the word! To your family, to technical person, at work...

What can I do?
As a web developer

  • Avoid direct inclusion of external content
  • Social buttons, video streaming...
  • Use Piwik instead of Google Analytics
  • Use Persona (connect with your email) instead of Facebook Connect / Google...
  • Don't host yourself on Cloud providers like Amazon
